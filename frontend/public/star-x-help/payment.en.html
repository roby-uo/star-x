star-X Payment Configuration

Payments are disabled by default. Enable them only after you have appropriate authority, merchant arrangements, tax and consumer-protection processes, and have reviewed your provider's terms.

This is an internal operational reminder for star-X, not legal, tax, financial, or payment-compliance advice.

Before enabling

  1. Define the service, price, refund, and customer-support policy.
  2. Use payment-provider credentials and receiving accounts that you control and are authorized to use.
  3. Test checkout, webhooks, refunds, and failure handling in a test environment.
  4. Restrict administrator access and reconcile orders with settlement records.

Current deployment

star-X does not charge users automatically unless an administrator configures both a payment provider and sale plans.
